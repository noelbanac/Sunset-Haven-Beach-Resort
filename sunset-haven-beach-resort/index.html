Your Home Away From the City

Sunset Haven Beach Resort is located at Mabini, Davao de Oro facing the Davao Gulf. We accept walk-ins for day tour, but you can also book your overnight stay especially during weekends or holidays to make sure you can avail our rooms. Come with your family, relatives and friends as you enjoy and experience the haven of relaxation, the gentle breeze of an unobstructed horizon, and a place where the sun meets the sea.

  • Chill out and experience the ocean breeze with its innumerable golden sand grains
  • Stay in our elegant, spotless rooms and cottages
  • Sing along with your loved ones and friends availing our karaoke section
  • Make the most of our function hall for special events like birthdays, debut party, wedding, conference, etc.
  • Explore our resort that is perfect for team building and family reunions
  • No entrance fee and no corkage fee
